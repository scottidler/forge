# Research Review Rubric

## Coverage
- [ ] Multiple sources are referenced (not just one perspective)
- [ ] Both advantages and disadvantages are explored
- [ ] Edge cases and limitations are identified
- [ ] Prior art and existing solutions are surveyed

## Analysis Quality
- [ ] Claims are supported with evidence or references
- [ ] Comparisons use consistent criteria
- [ ] Assumptions are explicitly stated
- [ ] Gaps in knowledge are acknowledged

## Actionability
- [ ] Key findings are summarized upfront
- [ ] Recommendations are specific and justified
- [ ] Next steps are clear
