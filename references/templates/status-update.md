# Status Update Template

## Structure

1. **TLDR** — One sentence summary of current state
2. **Accomplishments** — What was completed since last update (bullet points)
3. **In Progress** — What is actively being worked on
4. **Blockers** — What is preventing progress (with who can unblock)
5. **Next Steps** — What will be worked on next
6. **Metrics** — Any relevant numbers or measurements

## Guidelines

- Keep it under one page
- Use past tense for accomplishments, present for in-progress
- Be specific about blockers — name the dependency and the person
- Include dates for next steps when possible
